UPGRADED DYNASTY VALUE ALGORITHM (WORD FORMAT)

⸻

STEP 1 — MULTI-YEAR VALUE OVER REPLACEMENT (VOR)

For each player, calculate Value Over Replacement for the next 3 seasons based on the selected league setup.

Weight each season:

• Year 1 VOR = 50%
• Year 2 VOR = 30% and discounted by 8%
• Year 3 VOR = 20% and discounted by 15%

Add them together to create a Weighted VOR score.

⸻

STEP 2 — BASE DYNASTY VALUE

Normalize each player’s Weighted VOR onto a 0–100 scale relative to the top player in the league format.

This becomes the Base Value.

⸻

MULTIPLIERS (APPLIED TO BASE VALUE)

Multiply the Base Value by each of the following:

⸻

1. AGE MULTIPLIER (BY POSITION)

Use position peak ranges and yearly decay:

QB peak: 25–32 years, decay 3% per year after peak
RB peak: 22–27 years, decay 5% per year after peak
WR peak: 24–29 years, decay 4% per year after peak
TE peak: 25–30 years, decay 4% per year after peak

Players younger than peak can receive up to a 15% bonus.

Minimum age multiplier: 0.65
Maximum age multiplier: 1.15

⸻

2. ROLE SECURITY MULTIPLIER

Based on snap share, depth chart, and opportunity:

Elite locked-in starter: 1.10 to 1.15
Solid starter: 1.00 to 1.08
Committee player: 0.90 to 0.98
Backup: 0.75 to 0.88

⸻

3. INJURY RISK MULTIPLIER

Combine current injury status and historical durability.

Current status:

• IR or Out: 0.90
• Doubtful: 0.95
• Questionable: 0.98
• Healthy: 1.00

Historical injury risk:

• Durable: 1.00
• Moderate history: 0.97
• Frequent injuries: 0.93

Multiply both together.

⸻

4. PRODUCTION CEILING MULTIPLIER

Based on weekly fantasy points per game percentile:

Top 5% at position: 1.40 to 1.50
Top 15%: 1.20 to 1.30
Top 30%: 1.05 to 1.15
All others: 1.00

⸻

5. VOLATILITY MULTIPLIER

Based on weekly consistency:

Consistent producers: 1.03 to 1.08
Average volatility: 1.00
Boom/bust players: 0.90 to 0.97

⸻

6. DRAFT CAPITAL MULTIPLIER (FOR PLAYERS UNDER 4 YEARS IN LEAGUE)

NFL Draft round:

Round 1: 1.15
Round 2: 1.08
Round 3: 1.02
Day 3: 0.90
Undrafted: 0.85

Reduce this bonus by about 3% for each season played.

Once player is established, floor at 1.00.

⸻

7. TEAM CONTEXT MULTIPLIER

Based on offensive strength:

Top 5 offense: 1.05 to 1.08
Top 15 offense: 1.02 to 1.04
Bottom 10 offense: 0.95 to 0.98

⸻

STEP 3 — APPLY SMALL SCARCITY BONUS (OPTIONAL)

Since VOR already accounts for scarcity, only apply light bonuses for elite tiers:

Superflex leagues:

• Top 1–3 QBs: 8% to 12% boost

All leagues:

• Top 1–3 TEs: 10% to 15% boost
• Top 1–5 RBs in deep leagues: 5% to 10% boost

⸻

STEP 4 — DRAFT PICK VALUES

Base values:

1st round pick: 80
2nd round pick: 55
3rd round pick: 35
4th round pick: 18

Apply time decay:

Reduce value by about 10% for each year in the future.

Optionally boost early picks slightly in Superflex or TE premium leagues.

⸻

STEP 5 — MARKET CALIBRATION

Displayed dynasty value equals:

50% your calculated value
50% KTC consensus value

(You can shift to 60/40 later if desired)

⸻

STEP 6 — FINAL SCALING

Clamp all final values between 1 and 100.
